## Module 2: üíº Managing Jobs & Deals

### 2.1 The Kanban Board Explained
- **Columns**: Lead ‚Üí Contacted ‚Üí Booked ‚Üí In Progress ‚Üí Completed ‚Üí Paid
- **Cards**: Individual jobs with customer info and status
- **Drag & Drop**: Move cards between stages to update status

### 2.2 Creating a New Deal
**Manual Method:**
1. Click **"New Deal"** button
2. Fill customer details and job description
3. Set initial stage (usually "Lead")

**AI Method:**
```
"Create new deal for Sarah Johnson - electrical work needed"
```

### 2.3 Managing Stages (Drag & Drop)
- Click and hold any deal card
- Drag to appropriate column
- Release to update status automatically
- System logs all stage changes

### 2.4 Deal Details & Financials
Click any deal card to view:
- **Customer Info**: Contact details and history
- **Job Details**: Description and requirements
- **Financials**: Quote amount, final invoice, payment status
- **Timeline**: All activities and communications

### 2.5 Handling Stale & Rotting Deals
- **Stale Deals**: No activity for 7+ days (yellow warning)
- **Rotting Deals**: No activity for 14+ days (red alert)
- **Action Required**: Follow up or archive inactive deals

### 2.6 Quotes & Invoicing
1. **Quote**: Initial price estimate (editable)
2. **Final Invoice**: Actual amount after job completion
3. **Payment Tracking**: Mark as paid when received
4. **Adjustments**: Modify final amounts post-job if needed
