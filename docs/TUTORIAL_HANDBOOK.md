## 1. What is Earlymark?

Earlymark is a CRM and AI assistant built for Australian trade businesses — plumbers, electricians, builders, and any tradie who wants to win more leads and spend less time on admin.

At its core, Earlymark does three things:

1. **Tracks your jobs** from the first enquiry through to invoicing and payment.
2. **Communicates with customers** via SMS, email, and phone — automatically or with your approval.
3. **Learns your business** so the AI assistant (Tracey) can act on your behalf with confidence.

### Meet Tracey

Tracey is Earlymark's AI assistant. She appears in two places:

| Mode | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| **CRM Chatbot** (right panel) | Manages your CRM through natural language — create jobs, send texts, move deals, generate invoices |
| **Tracey Voice** | Answers or makes phone calls on behalf of your business, in your name |

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Sign Up

1. Go to the Earlymark homepage and click **Get Started**.
2. Sign up with your email/password, or continue with Google or GitHub.
3. Verify your phone number via SMS.

### 2.2 Create Your Workspace

After signing up, you'll be guided through a setup wizard:

1. **Business Name & Trade type** — e.g., "Smith Plumbing", Plumber.
2. **Contact details** — your business phone, email, and website.
3. **Service area** — the suburb you're based in and how far you'll travel (km radius).
4. **Working hours** — standard start/end times and whether you take emergency calls.

Take the time to fill this in accurately. Tracey uses this information every time she speaks to a customer.

### 2.3 Connect Your Phone Number

Go to **Settings → Workspace → Communications** and click **Set Up Phone**.

Earlymark will provision a dedicated Australian phone number for your workspace (via Twilio). Once connected:

- Inbound calls and texts are routed to your Earlymark inbox.
- Tracey can answer calls and send SMS on that number.

### 2.4 Connect Email (Optional but Recommended)

Go to **Settings → Integrations** and connect:

- **Gmail** or **Outlook** — to receive and send emails through Earlymark.
- Inbound leads from **HiPages**, **Airtasker**, and web forms can be auto-imported as deals once your email is connected.

## 3. The Dashboard

**Where to find it:** `Home` in the left sidebar.

The dashboard is your daily HQ. It shows:

### KPI Row (top of screen)

| Card | What it tells you |
|------|------------------|
| Jobs Today | How many jobs are scheduled for today |
| Revenue Today | Total invoiced value for today |
| Response Rate | % of new leads you've responded to |
| Completion Status | Jobs finished vs in-progress |

### Kanban Board (main area)

A drag-and-drop pipeline showing every job at its current stage. See [Section 4](#4-managing-jobs-with-the-kanban-board) for full details.

### Notifications Panel

Actionable alerts appear here — e.g., "Reminder: Job for Dave tomorrow at 9am, address is missing." Tap the action button directly on the notification.

### Morning Briefing

Each morning, Earlymark surfaces a preparation checklist for today's jobs:
- Jobs missing an address
- Customers with no phone number on file
- Unpaid invoices due today
- Any jobs without a confirmed booking

## 4. Managing Jobs with the Kanban Board

**Where to find it:** `Home` page, centre panel.

Every enquiry, lead, or job lives as a **Deal card** on the board.

### 4.1 Pipeline Stages

Drag cards left or right to move them through your pipeline:

| Stage | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **New** | Fresh lead just came in |
| **Contacted** | You or Tracey have reached out |
| **Negotiation** | Discussing price or scope |
| **Scheduled** | Job is booked in |
| **Pipeline** | On the backburner, not yet booked |
| **Invoiced** | Job done, invoice sent |
| **Pending Completion** | Waiting for final sign-off |
| **Won** | Paid and closed |
| **Lost** | Didn't win this one |
| **Archived** | Moved off the board |

### 4.2 Creating a Job

**Option A — Via the board:** Click the `+` button at the top of any column.

**Option B — Via Tracey:** Type in the chat panel:
> *"New job for Sarah Chen, blocked drain, quoting $400, she's in Sutherland"*

Tracey will create the deal and fill in the details automatically.

### 4.3 Job Card Details

Click any card to open the job detail panel:

- **Customer info** — name, phone, email
- **Job address** — with map pin (tap to open in Maps)
- **Value** — quoted or agreed price
- **Lead source** — website, HiPages, Airtasker, phone call, referral
- **Scheduled date/time**
- **Job status** — Scheduled → Travelling → On-Site → Completed
- **Photos** — upload before/after photos on the job
- **Notes** — internal notes visible only to your team
- **Activity log** — every SMS, call, email, and note automatically recorded

### 4.4 Bulk Actions

Long-press (hold) a card to enter selection mode. Select multiple cards, then:

- **Move** — shift them all to a new stage at once
- **Assign** — hand them to a team member
- **Disposition** — mark Won/Lost in bulk

### 4.5 Job Completion

When a job is done, open the deal and tap **Mark Complete**. A completion modal will appear:

1. Capture customer **signature** on screen
2. Log **materials used** (auto-cross-checked with your pricing glossary)
3. Collect **customer feedback** (star rating + comments)
4. **Create invoice** — pre-filled from job details

## 6. Inbox & Messaging

**Where to find it:** `Inbox` in the left sidebar.

The inbox is a unified view of all customer conversations — SMS, email, and system events — in one place.

### Inbox Layout

- **Left panel** — contact list with conversation previews. Filter by customer type, date, or unread.
- **Right panel** — the active conversation thread.

### Message Modes

At the top of the conversation, switch between:

| Mode | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **Direct Message** | You type, you send — a traditional messaging view |
| **Ask Tracey** | Type your intent and Tracey composes and sends the message |

### Ask Tracey in Inbox

With a conversation open, switch to Ask Tracey and type things like:
> *"Send a booking confirmation for tomorrow at 10am"*
> *"Ask them what type of hot water system they have"*
> *"Follow up on the unpaid invoice"*

Tracey will compose an appropriate SMS and either send it immediately or show it to you for approval, depending on your **Contact Mode** setting (see [Section 8.3](#83-contact-modes)).

### SMS & Email Templates

Go to **Settings → Templates** to save reusable messages:
- Booking confirmations
- "On my way" alerts
- Quote follow-ups
- Invoice reminders

## 7. Invoicing

**Where to find it:** Open any job card → **Invoice** tab, or ask Tracey to create one.

### Invoice Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | Not yet sent to customer |
| Issued | Sent and awaiting payment |
| Paid | Payment received |
| Void | Cancelled |

### Creating an Invoice

**Via job card:** Click **Create Invoice** after completing a job. Earlymark pre-fills the customer details and job value.

**Via Tracey:**
> *"Create an invoice for the Henderson job, $850 labour plus $120 materials"*

### Line Items

Add itemised line items for:
- **Labour** — time and hourly rate
- **Materials** — auto-priced from your pricing glossary where available

### Sending the Invoice

Click **Send Invoice** to email the invoice to the customer via Resend. An automatic reminder can be triggered if it goes unpaid.

### Xero Integration

If you connect Xero (Settings → Integrations), Earlymark will automatically create a draft invoice in Xero whenever you issue one here. No double entry needed.

## 8. Tracey — Your AI Assistant

Tracey is the right-hand panel that runs across every page. She can read and write anything in your CRM, draft and send communications, and answer questions about your schedule and business.

### 8.1 What Tracey Can Do

**Jobs & Deals**
- Create single or multiple jobs from one message
- Move deals between pipeline stages
- Assign jobs to team members
- Update job details (address, value, date)
- Bulk-move or bulk-close deals

**Contacts**
- Look up customers by name, phone, or address
- Create new contact records
- Merge duplicate contacts

**Invoices**
- Create, update, and mark invoices as paid
- Generate invoices from job summaries

**Communication**
- Send SMS to customers
- Send emails
- Initiate outbound calls (via Tracey Voice)

**Scheduling**
- Tell you what's on tomorrow
- Find gaps in the schedule
- Set job reminders

**Reporting**
- Revenue summaries
- Job counts and close rates
- Outstanding invoices

### 8.2 Example Commands

You don't need to memorise any syntax — just speak naturally:

| What you want | What to type |
|---------------|-------------|
| New job | *"Job for Mike, hot water system replacement, Caringbah, quote $1,200"* |
| Multiple jobs at once | *"3 jobs today: Tom at 9am broken tap $200, Sarah at 1pm blocked drain $350, Liz at 3pm inspection $150"* |
| Move a deal | *"Move the Henderson job to Scheduled"* |
| Send a text | *"Text Dave to confirm tomorrow at 8am"* |
| Create invoice | *"Invoice the Smith job, $750"* |
| Check schedule | *"What have I got on this week?"* |
| Revenue report | *"How much have I made this month?"* |
| Bulk close | *"Mark all Won jobs from last month as archived"* |

### 8.3 Contact Modes

Control how proactively Tracey communicates with your customers. Set this in **Settings → Workspace → Agent Mode**.

| Mode | What happens |
|------|-------------|
| **Execute** | Tracey contacts customers automatically without asking you first |
| **Review & Approve** | Tracey drafts messages and waits for your thumbs-up before sending |
| **Info Only** | Tracey gives you information and suggestions but never contacts customers directly |

### 8.4 Tracey's Knowledge Base

Tracey knows your business because you've told her. Manage this in **Settings → Business Profile**:

- **Services** — what you offer and at what price
- **No-go rules** — jobs you never take (e.g., "No asbestos removal", "No jobs under $100")
- **Service rules** — conditions you always apply (e.g., "Always charge call-out fee on weekends")
- **Working hours** — including emergency availability

The more detail you add here, the better Tracey performs.

## 9. Voice & Calls

Earlymark includes a full voice system powered by LiveKit, Deepgram, and Cartesia. Tracey can answer and make phone calls using a natural, human-like voice.

### 9.1 Inbound Calls

When a customer calls your Earlymark number:

1. Tracey answers the call.
2. She introduces herself and asks how she can help.
3. She can book jobs, answer pricing questions, and take messages.
4. After the call, a transcript and summary are saved to the matching contact/deal automatically.

### 9.2 Outbound Calls

Ask Tracey to call a customer:
> *"Call Dave Henderson about tomorrow's job"*

Tracey will initiate the call and handle the conversation. You'll get a full transcript when it ends.

### 9.3 Call Logs

Every call is stored under the relevant contact or deal with:
- Full conversation transcript
- Call summary
- Duration
- Latency metrics (for your tech curiosity)

### 9.4 Demo Calls

Want to see Tracey in action? Click **Request Demo Call** from the homepage. A call will be placed to your phone within seconds. Tracey will walk through a sample enquiry as if she were answering for your business.

## 13. Automations & Workflows

Earlymark handles a lot of repetitive tasks automatically.

### Lead Capture

| Source | How it works |
|--------|-------------|
| **Phone call** | Tracey answers and creates a deal automatically |
| **SMS** | Inbound texts are matched to existing contacts or create a new lead |
| **Email** | Inbound emails (including HiPages/Airtasker) are parsed and imported as deals |
| **Webform** | Connect your website form via webhook — submissions become deals instantly |

### Stale Lead Recovery

If a lead sits in **New** or **Contacted** for too long without a response, Earlymark can automatically trigger a follow-up SMS. Configure the timing in **Settings → Automations**.

### The Triage Engine ("The Bouncer")

Before Tracey books or quotes a job, she checks it against your no-go rules:

- **Hard no-go** — Tracey declines the job and explains why to the customer.
- **Soft flag** — Tracey flags it to you but can still accept the job with your approval.

This prevents Tracey from committing you to work you don't want.

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of Earlymark

**Set up your knowledge base first.** The more Tracey knows about your services, pricing, and rules, the better she performs. Spend 10 minutes in Settings → Business Profile before your first call.

**Use Review & Approve mode when you're starting out.** Once you're confident Tracey understands your voice and style, switch to Execute mode to fully automate customer contact.

**Keep job addresses up to date.** Tracey will alert you if an address is missing before a job day. The map view and travel planning both depend on this.

**Let inbound emails flow in.** Connecting Gmail or Outlook means leads from HiPages, Airtasker, and your website land directly in your pipeline as deals — no manual entry.

**Use bulk actions for housekeeping.** At the end of the month, select all Won jobs and archive them in one tap to keep the board clean.
